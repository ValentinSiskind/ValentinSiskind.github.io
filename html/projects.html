Connect-4

The aim of the game is simple: to get 4 pieces in a line. Each player can place 1 piece every turn, and the pieces fall to the bottom of the board like in the real game, simulating gravity. In this version of Connect-4, you type the column that you want your piece to fall in, and press enter. Then watch as your piece magically appears on the board! Once a player achieves the goal, or there is a tie, the game ends. Click here to view this project >

Hangman

To play hangman, a random word is picked from a list of over 10,000 words, and you must guess 1 letter at a time, unti you guess the word, or run out of guess. After the game is over, the program asks you if you want to play again, and if you say no, it tells you what your win percentage was for that session. Click here to view this project >

World Cup 1978 Short Film

This is a short film portraying the final of the 1978 World Cup Final between Argentina and the Netherlands. For this assignment, our task was to incorporate some sort of magical realism into our project, and this was done when the ball hits the boy's head, you don't know if they all actually got transported back in time, or if he just imagined it. At the end of the film, homage is payed to the victims of the Argentinian dictatorhsip that ruled over the country from 1976 to 1983, murdering and disapearing over 30,000 people.

Biology Rap

This rap and music video were produced for biology class, and most of the images used were taken from the Harvard Natural History museum. The video encompasses four secions of the museums, namely evolution, arthropods, ocean life, and mammals.
