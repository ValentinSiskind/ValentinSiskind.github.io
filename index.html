A text based version of the classic game Hangman, coded in Python. To play hangman, a random word is picked from a list of over 10,000, ranging from 3 to 10 letters long. The player must guess 1 letter at a time, until they guess the word, or run out of guesses. After the game is over, the program asks the player if they want to play again, and tells them their win percentage for that session.
